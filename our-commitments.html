Commitment to Sustainability

DonalFarms takes its environmental responsibilities seriously. It is our policy to continually improve our farming systems and working standards
to meet our environmental, legal and social responsibilities. We have therefore participated in numerous schemes and initiatives.

Other Commitments

  • Plan the farms crop rotation and variety choice to minimise use of fertilisers, pesticides, herbicides and fungicides.

  • Umbilical slurry injection cuts our fertiliser down tenfold.

  • Maintain and enhance the structure of our soil through sustainable crop rotations and well managed operations.

  • Thoroughly research the source of our products, buying from nurseries and manufacturers who adhere to high quality and environmental standards.

  • Continually make sure our products come from sustainable sources, such as FSC certified woods and Fairtrade coffee in our cafes.

  • Recommend alternatives to chemical options where possible.
